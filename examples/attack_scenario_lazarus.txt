INCIDENT REPORT: Financial Institution Breach
Date: March 10-31, 2026
Target: Regional Bank - Financial Services Division

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY:
On March 10, 2026, our security operations center detected suspicious activity
originating from the financial services division. Initial investigation revealed
a sophisticated multi-stage attack targeting cryptocurrency wallet management
systems and SWIFT transaction processing infrastructure.

ATTACK TIMELINE:

Week 1 (March 10-16):
- Initial access via spearphishing email to finance department employees
- Malicious Word document with embedded VBA macros
- Subject line: "Q1 2026 Cryptocurrency Market Analysis Report"
- Macro executed PowerShell payload upon document opening
- PowerShell loader established persistence via registry Run keys
- No immediate C2 communication observed (48-hour sleep period)

Week 2 (March 17-23):
- Loader activated and established HTTPS C2 channel
- C2 domain: financial-analytics[.]com (masquerading as legitimate service)
- Beacon interval: 4-6 hours with random jitter
- Lateral movement observed via RDP to financial servers
- Credential dumping detected: LSASS memory access by non-system process
- Pass-the-hash technique used for privilege escalation

Week 3 (March 24-31):
- Attacker gained access to cryptocurrency wallet management system
- Attempted access to SWIFT transaction terminals (blocked by network segmentation)
- Data staging observed: 2.3GB of financial records compressed with 7-zip
- Exfiltration to cloud storage service (OneDrive) over 5-day period
- Small file sizes (50-100KB chunks) to evade DLP detection
- Exfiltration occurred during business hours to blend with normal traffic

TECHNICAL INDICATORS:
- Long dwell time (3 weeks) before detection
- Sophisticated evasion: business-hours only activity, low beacon frequency
- Financial sector targeting with specific focus on cryptocurrency infrastructure
- Use of legitimate cloud services for exfiltration
- Professional operational security: no noisy tools, minimal forensic artifacts

IMPACT ASSESSMENT:
- Confidential financial records compromised
- Cryptocurrency wallet credentials potentially exposed
- No direct financial loss detected (exfiltration only, no fund transfer)
- Estimated attacker dwell time: 21 days

ANALYST NOTES:
The sophistication level, financial targeting, cryptocurrency focus, and
operational patterns are consistent with nation-state APT activity rather
than common cybercrime. The long dwell time and careful operational security
suggest intelligence collection or pre-positioning for future operations.
